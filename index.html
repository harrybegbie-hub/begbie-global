Why We Exist

Built Around the Problems Students Actually Face

Every part of a Begbie Global package exists because a real student needed it and didn’t have it. Let the packages speak for themselves.

✈

Nobody explains UK public transport

Multiple operators, ticket types and connections — after a long-haul flight, alone, with heavy bags. We arrange the ticket before they board so they just follow the plan.

🏠

An empty room on day one is daunting

Sourcing bedding and kitchen basics in an unfamiliar city, after a long journey, often late at night — it’s the first thing students dread. Their essentials are already waiting.

📲

No signal, no data, no idea where to start

Landing without a working UK number is disorienting. Every international package includes an e-SIM so they’re connected from the moment the plane touches down.

📚

UK admin is confusing even for locals

GP registration, university enrolment, bank accounts, guarantor requirements — none of it is intuitive from abroad. We guide students through every step, clearly, before they arrive.

💼

Getting a job in the UK is its own challenge

Visa working restrictions, UK CV structure, what interviewers expect — students who know this are significantly ahead of those who don’t. We make sure they know.

👪

Families worry from thousands of miles away

Because everything is confirmed before departure, families can see exactly what’s in place. Our UK-based team is contactable throughout so nobody is left wondering.

FAQ

Questions About How We Handle Everything

Everything you need to know before you book.

We arrange delivery directly to your accommodation address — timed to arrive before you do. Whether you're moving into university halls or a private flat, everything is waiting in your room when you walk through the door. No collecting parcels, no carrying boxes, nothing to worry about on day one.
Every package includes bedding, towels, and kitchen essentials as a minimum. International packages also include a UK e-SIM (so you're connected the moment you land), a public transport ticket from your arrival airport to your university city, and full arrival guidance. Premium packages upgrade the kitchen items and add stationery, toiletries, laundry essentials, and priority support. You can see exactly what's in each package above — and add extras with our optional add-ons.
Tell us your arrival airport, date and time when you book. We arrange your train or coach ticket from the airport to your university city — the cost is covered up to your package's included transport credit. If your route costs more, we show you the difference clearly before you pay. A private door-to-door airport taxi is also available as an add-on, priced by route using real UK rates.
An e-SIM is a digital SIM card that is activated on your phone before you travel — no physical card needed. All international packages include a UK e-SIM, so you'll have a working UK mobile number and data the moment your plane lands. No hunting for a SIM shop at the airport, no expensive roaming charges.
We recommend booking at least 4 weeks before your arrival date. This gives us enough time to prepare everything and ensures your preferred bedding colours are available. If your arrival is sooner, get in touch at [email protected] — we'll always do our best to help.
Absolutely — many of our bookings come from parents and guardians. Just provide the student's name, accommodation address and arrival date. We'll handle everything from there and keep you updated throughout.
UK packages are paid in full at booking by card via Stripe — we accept all major cards including international cards. International packages can be paid in full or with a deposit — pay half now to secure your place, then the balance before your services begin. If your visa is still pending, the deposit option lets you hold your place until it's approved. If you'd prefer to pay by bank transfer, just contact us on WhatsApp or email and we'll arrange it.
If your visa is formally refused, contact us at [email protected] with your official refusal letter. We review deposit refunds in line with our Refund Policy. We always aim to be fair — if we haven't yet started significant work on your package, we'll do our best to help.
Contact us as soon as possible by email or WhatsApp. If your arrival date or accommodation changes, we'll do everything we can to update arrangements. The sooner you let us know, the more we can do. Please review our Terms & Conditions and Refund Policy for full details.
We cover all major UK universities nationwide — from Chester and Manchester to London, Edinburgh, Birmingham, and beyond. Not sure if we cover yours? Just ask. We're always expanding and will do our best to accommodate you wherever you're studying.
